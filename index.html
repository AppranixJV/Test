Historical Events for 10/24

  • Year: 2016 - A French surveillance aircraft flying to Libya crashes on takeoff in Malta, killing all five people on board.
  • Year: 2015 - A driver crashes into the Oklahoma State Homecoming parade, killing four people and injuring 34.
  • Year: 2014 - The China National Space Administration launches an experimental lunar mission, Chang'e 5-T1, which will loop behind the Moon and return to Earth.
  • Year: 2008 - "Bloody Friday" saw many of the world's stock exchanges experience the worst declines in their history, with drops of around 10% in most indices.
  • Year: 2007 - Chang'e 1, the first satellite in the Chinese Lunar Exploration Program, is launched from Xichang Satellite Launch Center.
  • Year: 2005 - Hurricane Wilma makes landfall in Florida, resulting in 35 direct and 26 indirect fatalities and causing $20.6B USD in damage.

  • Year: 2003 - Concorde makes its last commercial flight.

  • Year: 1992 - The Toronto Blue Jays become the first Major League Baseball team based outside the United States to win the World Series.
  • Year: 1990 - Italian prime minister Giulio Andreotti reveals to the Italian parliament the existence of Gladio, the Italian NATO force formed in 1956, intended to be activated in the event of a Warsaw Pact invasion.

  • Year: 1980 - The government of Poland legalizes the Solidarity trade union.
  • Year: 1975 - In Iceland, 90% of women take part in a national strike, refusing to work in protest of gender inequality.
  • Year: 1964 - Northern Rhodesia gains independence from the United Kingdom and becomes Zambia.
  • Year: 1963 - An oxygen leak from an R-9 Desna missile at the Baikonur Cosmodrome triggers a fire that kills seven people.
  • Year: 1960 - Nedelin catastrophe: An R-16 ballistic missile explodes on the launch pad at the Soviet Union's Baikonur Cosmodrome space facility, killing over 100 people, including Field Marshal Mitrofan Nedelin.
  • Year: 1957 - The United States Air Force starts the X-20 Dyna-Soar crewed space program.
  • Year: 1954 - US President Dwight D. Eisenhower pledges United States support to South Vietnam.
  • Year: 1949 - The cornerstone of the United Nations Headquarters is laid.

  • Year: 1946 - A camera on board the V-2 No. 13 rocket takes the first photograph of earth from outer space.
  • Year: 1945 - The United Nations Charter comes into effect.
  • Year: 1944 - World War II: Japan's center force is temporarily repulsed in the Battle of Leyte Gulf.
  • Year: 1931 - The George Washington Bridge opens to public traffic over the Hudson River.
  • Year: 1930 - A bloodless coup d'état in Brazil ends the First Republic, replacing it with the Vargas Era.
  • Year: 1929 - "Black Thursday" on the New York Stock Exchange.
  • Year: 1926 - Harry Houdini's last performance takes place at the Garrick Theatre in Detroit.
  • Year: 1918 - World War I: Italian victory in the Battle of Vittorio Veneto.
  • Year: 1917 - World War I: Italy suffers a disastrous defeat at the Battle of Caporetto on the Austro-Italian front.
  • Year: 1912 - First Balkan War: The Battle of Kumanovo concludes with the Serbian victory against the Ottoman Empire.
  • Year: 1912 - First Balkan War: The Battle of Kirk Kilisse concludes with a Bulgarian victory against the Ottoman Empire.

  • Year: 1902 - Guatemala's Santa María Volcano begins to erupt, becoming the third-largest eruption of the 20th century.
