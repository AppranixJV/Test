Historical Events for 09/29

  • Year: 2019 - Violence and low turnout mar the 2019 Afghan presidential election.
  • Year: 2016 - Eleven days after the Uri attack, the Indian Army conducts "surgical strikes" against suspected militants in Pakistani-administered Kashmir.
  • Year: 2013 - Over 42 people are killed by members of Boko Haram at the College of Agriculture in Nigeria.

  • Year: 1932 - Last day of the Battle of Boquerón between Paraguay and Bolivia during the Chaco War.
