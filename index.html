Historical Events for 10/01

  • Year: 2019 - Kuopio school stabbing: one dies and ten are injured when Joel Marin, armed with a sabre, attacks a school class at Savo Vocational College in Kuopio, Finland.
  • Year: 2018 - The International Court of Justice rules that Chile is not obliged to negotiate access to the Pacific Ocean with Bolivia.
  • Year: 2017 - Fifty-eight people are killed and 869 others injured in a mass shooting at a country music festival at the Las Vegas Strip in the United States; the gunman, Stephen Paddock, later commits suicide.
  • Year: 2017 - An independence referendum, later declared illegal by the Constitutional Court of Spain, takes place in Catalonia.
  • Year: 2015 - The American cargo vessel SS El Faro sinks with all of its 33 crew after steaming into the eyewall of Hurricane Joaquin.
  • Year: 2015 - Heavy rains trigger a major landslide in Guatemala, killing 280 people.
  • Year: 2015 - A gunman kills nine people at a community college in Oregon.
  • Year: 2014 - A double bombing of an elementary school in Homs, Syria kills over 50 people.
  • Year: 2014 - A series of explosions at a gunpowder plant in Bulgaria completely destroys the factory, killing 15 people.
  • Year: 2012 - A ferry collision off the coast of Hong Kong kills 38 people and injures 102 others.
  • Year: 2009 - The Supreme Court of the United Kingdom takes over the judicial functions of the House of Lords.
  • Year: 2001 - Militants attack the state legislature building in Kashmir, killing 38.
  • Year: 2000 - Israel-Palestinian conflict: Palestinians protest the murder of 12-year-old Muhammad al-Durrah by Israeli police in northern Israel, beginning the "October 2000 events".
  • Year: 1994 - Palau enters a Compact of Free Association with the United States.
  • Year: 1991 - Croatian War of Independence: The Siege of Dubrovnik begins.
  • Year: 1989 - Denmark introduces the world's first legal same-sex registered partnerships.
  • Year: 1987 - The 5.9 Mw  Whittier Narrows earthquake shakes the San Gabriel Valley with a Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe), killing eight and injuring 200.
  • Year: 1985 - Israel-Palestinian conflict: Israel attacks the Palestine Liberation Organization's Tunisia headquarters during Operation Wooden Leg.
  • Year: 1982 - Sony and Phillips launch the compact disc in Japan; on the same day, Sony releases the model CDP-101 compact disc player, the first player of its kind.
  • Year: 1982 - EPCOT (Experimental Prototype Community of Tomorrow) opens at Walt Disney World in Florida.
  • Year: 1982 - Helmut Kohl replaces Helmut Schmidt as Chancellor of Germany through a constructive vote of no confidence.
  • Year: 1979 - The MTR, Hong Kong's rapid transit railway system, opens.
  • Year: 1979 - Pope John Paul II begins his first pastoral visit to the United States.
  • Year: 1978 - Tuvalu gains independence from the United Kingdom.
  • Year: 1975 - Muhammad Ali defeats Joe Frazier in a boxing match in Manila, Philippines.
  • Year: 1971 - The first practical CT scanner is used to diagnose a patient.
  • Year: 1971 - Walt Disney World opens near Orlando, Florida.
  • Year: 1969 - Concorde breaks the sound barrier for the first time.
  • Year: 1968 - Guyana nationalizes the British Guiana Broadcasting Service, which would eventually become part of the National Communications Network, Guyana.
  • Year: 1966 - West Coast Airlines Flight 956 crashes with no survivors in Oregon. This accident marks the first loss of a DC-9.
  • Year: 1964 - Japanese Shinkansen ("bullet trains") begin high-speed rail service from Tokyo to Osaka.
  • Year: 1964 - The Free Speech Movement is launched on the campus of the University of California, Berkeley.
  • Year: 1962 - James Meredith enters the University of Mississippi, defying racial segregation rules.
  • Year: 1961 - The CTV Television Network, Canada's first private television network, is launched.
  • Year: 1961 - East and West Cameroon merge to form the Federal Republic of Cameroon.
  • Year: 1961 - The United States Defense Intelligence Agency is formed, becoming the country's first centralized military intelligence organization.
  • Year: 1960 - Nigeria gains independence from the United Kingdom.
  • Year: 1958 - The National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics is replaced by NASA.
  • Year: 1957 - The motto In God We Trust first appears on U.S. paper currency.
  • Year: 1955 - The Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region is established.
  • Year: 1953 - A United States-South Korea mutual defense treaty is concluded in Washington, D.C.
  • Year: 1953 - Andhra State is formed, consisting of a Telugu-speaking area carved out of India's Madras State.
  • Year: 1949 - The People's Republic of China is established.
  • Year: 1947 - The North American F-86 Sabre flies for the first time.
  • Year: 1946 - The Daegu October incident occurs in Allied-occupied Korea.
  • Year: 1946 - Nazi leaders are sentenced at the Nuremberg trials.
  • Year: 1943 - World War II: After the Four Days of Naples, Allied troops enter the city.
  • Year: 1942 - World War II: USS Grouper torpedoes Lisbon Maru, not knowing that she is carrying British prisoners of war from Hong Kong.
  • Year: 1940 - The Pennsylvania Turnpike, often considered the first superhighway in the United States, opens to traffic.
  • Year: 1939 - World War II: After a one-month siege, German troops occupy Warsaw.
  • Year: 1938 - Pursuant to the Munich Agreement signed the day before, Nazi Germany begins the military occupation and annexation of Czechoslovakia's Sudetenland.
  • Year: 1936 - Spanish Civil War: The Central Committee of Antifascist Militias of Catalonia dissolves itself, handing control of Catalan defence militias over to the Generalitat.
  • Year: 1936 - Spanish Civil War: Francisco Franco is named head of the Nationalist government of Spain.
  • Year: 1931 - Clara Campoamor persuades the Constituent Cortes to enfranchise women in Spain's new constitution.
  • Year: 1931 - The George Washington Bridge in the United States is opened, linking New Jersey and New York.
  • Year: 1928 - Newark Liberty International Airport opens, becoming the first airport in the New York City metro area.
  • Year: 1928 - The Soviet Union introduces its first five-year plan.
  • Year: 1918 - Sayid Abdullah becomes the last Khan of Khiva.
  • Year: 1918 - World War I: The Egyptian Expeditionary Force captures Damascus.
  • Year: 1910 - A large bomb destroys the Los Angeles Times building, killing 21.
  • Year: 1908 - Ford Model T automobiles are offered for sale at a price of US$825.
  • Year: 1903 - Baseball: The Boston Americans play the Pittsburgh Pirates in the first game of the modern World Series.
  • Year: 1898 - The Vienna University of Economics and Business Administration is founded under the name k.u.k. Exportakademie.
  • Year: 1891 - Stanford University opens its doors in California, United States.
  • Year: 1890 - Yosemite National Park is established by the U.S. Congress.
  • Year: 1887 - Balochistan is conquered by the British Empire.
  • Year: 1861 - Mrs Beeton's Book of Household Management is published, going on to sell 60,000 copies in its first year and remaining in print until the present day.
  • Year: 1832 - Texian political delegates convene at San Felipe de Austin to petition for changes in the governance of Mexican Texas.
  • Year: 1829 - The South African College is founded in Cape Town, later separating into the University of Cape Town and the South African College Schools.
  • Year: 1827 - Russo-Persian War: The Russian army under Ivan Paskevich storms Yerevan, ending a millennium of Muslim domination of Armenia.
  • Year: 1814 - The Congress of Vienna opens with the intention of redrawing Europe's political map after the defeat of Napoleon in the previous spring.
  • Year: 1800 - Via the Third Treaty of San Ildefonso, Spain cedes Louisiana to France, which would sell the land to the United States thirty months later.

  • Year: 1779 - The city of Tampere, Finland (belonging to Sweden at this time) is founded by King Gustav III of Sweden.
  • Year: 1730 - Ahmed III is forced to abdicate as the Ottoman sultan.
  • Year: 1588 - The coronation of Shah Abbas I of Persia occurs.
  • Year: 1553 - The coronation of Queen Mary I of England is held at Westminster Abbey.
  • Year: 965 - Pope John XIII is consecrated.
  • Year: 959 - Edgar the Peaceful becomes king of all England, in succession to Eadwig.
  • Year: 366 - Pope Damasus I is consecrated.
  • Year: 331 BC - Alexander the Great defeats Darius III of Persia in the Battle of Gaugamela.
