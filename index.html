Historical Events for 02/16

  • Year: 2021 - Five thousand people gathered in the town of Kherrata, Bejaia Province to mark the two year anniversary of the Hirak protest movement. Demonstrations had been suspended because of the COVID-19 pandemic in Algeria.
  • Year: 2013 - A bomb blast at a market in Hazara Town, Quetta, Pakistan kills more than 80 people and injures 190 others.
  • Year: 2006 - The last Mobile army surgical hospital (MASH) is decommissioned by the United States Army.
  • Year: 2005 - The National Hockey League cancels the entire 2004-05 regular season and playoffs.
  • Year: 2005 - The Kyoto Protocol comes into force, following its ratification by Russia.
  • Year: 2000 - Emery Worldwide Airlines Flight 17 crashes near Sacramento Mather Airport in Rancho Cordova, California, killing all three aboard.
  • Year: 1998 - China Airlines Flight 676 crashes into a road and residential area near Chiang Kai-shek International Airport in Taiwan, killing all 196 aboard and seven more on the ground.
  • Year: 1996 - A Chicago-bound Amtrak train, the Capitol Limited, collides with a MARC commuter train bound for Washington, D.C., killing 11 people.
  • Year: 1991 - Nicaraguan Contras leader Enrique Bermúdez is assassinated in Managua.
  • Year: 1986 - China Airlines Flight 2265 crashes into the Pacific Ocean near Penghu Airport in Taiwan, killing all 13 aboard.
  • Year: 1986 - The Soviet liner MS Mikhail Lermontov runs aground in the Marlborough Sounds, New Zealand.
  • Year: 1985 - Hezbollah is founded.
  • Year: 1983 - The Ash Wednesday bushfires in Victoria and South Australia kill 75.
  • Year: 1978 - The first computer bulletin board system is created (CBBS in Chicago).
  • Year: 1968 - Civil Air Transport Flight 010 crashes near Shongshan Airport in Taiwan, killing 21 of the 63 people on board and one more on the ground.
  • Year: 1968 - In Haleyville, Alabama, the first 9-1-1 emergency telephone system goes into service.
  • Year: 1962 - Flooding in the coastal areas of West Germany kills 315 and destroys the homes of about 60,000 people.
  • Year: 1962 - The Great Sheffield Gale impacts the United Kingdom, killing nine people; the city of Sheffield is devastated, with 150,000 homes damaged.
  • Year: 1961 - Explorer program: Explorer 9 (S-56a) is launched.
  • Year: 1960 - The U.S. Navy submarine USS Triton begins Operation Sandblast, setting sail from New London, Connecticut, to begin the first submerged circumnavigation of the globe.
  • Year: 1959 - Fidel Castro becomes Premier of Cuba after dictator Fulgencio Batista was overthrown on January 1.
  • Year: 1945 - The Alaska Equal Rights Act of 1945, the first anti-discrimination law in the United States, was signed into law.
  • Year: 1945 - World War II: American forces land on Corregidor Island in the Philippines.
  • Year: 1943 - World War II: In the early phases of the Third Battle of Kharkov, Red Army troops re-enter the city.
  • Year: 1942 - World War II: Attack on Aruba, first World War II German shots fired on a land based object in the Americas.
  • Year: 1942 - World War II: In Athens, the Greek People's Liberation Army is established
  • Year: 1940 - World War II: Altmark incident: The German tanker Altmark is boarded by sailors from the British destroyer HMS Cossack. A total of 299 British prisoners are freed.
  • Year: 1937 - Wallace H. Carothers receives a United States patent for nylon.
  • Year: 1936 - The Popular Front wins the 1936 Spanish general election.
  • Year: 1934 - The Austrian Civil War ends with the defeat of the Social Democrats and the Republikanischer Schutzbund.
  • Year: 1930 - The Romanian Football Federation joins FIFA.
  • Year: 1923 - Howard Carter unseals the burial chamber of Pharaoh Tutankhamun.
  • Year: 1918 - The Council of Lithuania unanimously adopts the Act of Independence, declaring Lithuania an independent state.
  • Year: 1900 - The Southern Cross expedition led by Carsten Borchgrevink achieved a new Farthest South of 78° 50'S, making the first landing at the Great Ice Barrier.
  • Year: 1899 - Iceland's first football club, Knattspyrnufélag Reykjavíkur, is founded.
  • Year: 1881 - The Canadian Pacific Railway is incorporated by Act of Parliament at Ottawa (44th Vic., c.1).
  • Year: 1866 - Spencer Compton Cavendish, Marquess of Hartington becomes British Secretary of State for War.
  • Year: 1862 - American Civil War: General Ulysses S. Grant captures Fort Donelson, Tennessee.
  • Year: 1804 - First Barbary War: Stephen Decatur leads a raid to burn the pirate-held frigate USS Philadelphia.
  • Year: 1796 - Colombo in Ceylon (now Sri Lanka) falls to the British, completing their invasion of Ceylon.
  • Year: 1742 - Spencer Compton, Earl of Wilmington, becomes British Prime Minister.
  • Year: 1699 - First Leopoldine Diploma is issued by the Holy Roman Emperor, recognizing the Greek Catholic clergy enjoyed the same privileges as Roman Catholic priests in the Principality of Transylvania.
  • Year: 1646 - Battle of Torrington, Devon: The last major battle of the First English Civil War.
  • Year: 1630 - Dutch forces led by Hendrick Lonck capture Olinda in what was to become part of Dutch Brazil.
  • Year: 1270 - Grand Duchy of Lithuania defeats the Livonian Order in the Battle of Karuse.
  • Year: 1249 - Andrew of Longjumeau is dispatched by Louis IX of France as his ambassador to meet with the Khagan of the Mongol Empire.
