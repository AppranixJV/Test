Historical Events for 11/29

  • Year: 2013 - LAM Mozambique Airlines 470 crashes in the Bwabata National Park in a pilot mass murder-suicide, killing all 33 people on board.
  • Year: 2009 - Maurice Clemmons shoots and kills four police officers inside a coffee shop in Lakewood, Washington.
  • Year: 2007 - The Armed Forces of the Philippines lay siege to the Peninsula Manila after soldiers led by Senator Antonio Trillanes stage a mutiny.
  • Year: 1987 - North Korean agents plant a bomb on Korean Air Flight 858, which kills all 115 passengers and crew.
  • Year: 1986 - The Surinamese military attacks the village of Moiwana during the Suriname Guerrilla War, killing at least 39 civilians, mostly women and children.
  • Year: 1972 - Atari releases Pong, the first commercially successful video game.
  • Year: 1967 - U.S. Secretary of Defense Robert McNamara announces his resignation.
  • Year: 1963 - "I Want to Hold Your Hand", recorded on October 17, 1963, is released by the Beatles in the United Kingdom.
  • Year: 1963 - Trans-Canada Air Lines Flight 831 crashes shortly after takeoff from Montreal-Dorval International Airport, killing all 118 people on board.
  • Year: 1963 - U.S. President Lyndon B. Johnson establishes the Warren Commission to investigate the assassination of President John F. Kennedy.
  • Year: 1961 - Enos, a chimpanzee, is launched into space. The spacecraft orbits the Earth twice and splashes down off the coast of Puerto Rico.
  • Year: 1952 - U.S. President-elect Dwight D. Eisenhower fulfills a campaign promise by traveling to Korea to find out what can be done to end the conflict.
  • Year: 1947 - French forces carry out a massacre at Mỹ Trạch, Vietnam during the First Indochina War.
  • Year: 1947 - The United Nations General Assembly approves a plan for the partition of Palestine.
  • Year: 1945 - The Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia is declared.
  • Year: 1944 - Albania is liberated by the Partisans.
  • Year: 1943 - The second session of the Anti-Fascist Council for the National Liberation of Yugoslavia (AVNOJ), held to determine the post-war ordering of the country, concludes in Jajce (present-day Bosnia and Herzegovina).
  • Year: 1929 - U.S. Admiral Richard E. Byrd leads the first expedition to fly over the South Pole.
  • Year: 1899 - FC Barcelona is founded by Catalan, Spanish and Englishmen. It later develops into one of Spanish football's most iconic and strongest teams.
  • Year: 1890 - The Meiji Constitution goes into effect in Japan, and the first Diet convenes.
  • Year: 1877 - Thomas Edison demonstrates his phonograph for the first time.
  • Year: 1872 - The Modoc War begins with the Battle of Lost River.
  • Year: 1864 - The Confederate Army of Tennessee misses an opportunity to crush the Army of the Ohio in the Battle of Spring Hill.
  • Year: 1864 - Colorado volunteers led by Colonel John Chivington massacre at least 150 Cheyenne and Arapaho noncombatants inside Colorado Territory.
  • Year: 1863 - Union forces under Ambrose Burnside successfully defend Knoxville, Tennessee from Confederate forces under James Longstreet in the Battle of Fort Sanders in the American Civil War.
  • Year: 1850 - The treaty, Punctation of Olmütz, is signed in Olomouc. Prussia capitulates to Austria, which will take over the leadership of the German Confederation.
  • Year: 1847 - Missionaries Dr. Marcus Whitman, his wife Narcissa, and 15 others are killed by Cayuse and Umatilla Indians, causing the Cayuse War.
  • Year: 1847 - The Sonderbund is defeated by the joint forces of other Swiss cantons under General Guillaume-Henri Dufour.
  • Year: 1830 - An armed rebellion against Russia's rule in Poland begins.
  • Year: 1807 - John VI of Portugal flees Lisbon from advancing Napoleonic forces during the Peninsular War, transferring the Portuguese court to Brazil.
  • Year: 1783 - A 5.3 magnitude earthquake strikes New Jersey.
  • Year: 1781 - The crew of the British slave ship Zong murders 54 Africans by dumping them into the sea to claim insurance, beginning the Zong massacre.
  • Year: 1777 - San Jose, California, is founded as Pueblo de San José de Guadalupe by José Joaquín Moraga. It is the first civilian settlement, or pueblo, in Alta California.
  • Year: 1776 - During the American Revolutionary War, the Battle of Fort Cumberland, Nova Scotia, comes to an end with the arrival of British reinforcements.
  • Year: 1732 - The magnitude 6.6 Irpinia earthquake causes 1,940 deaths in the former Kingdom of Naples, southern Italy.
  • Year: 1729 - Natchez Indians massacre 138 Frenchmen, 35 French women, and 56 children at Fort Rosalie, near the site of modern-day Natchez, Mississippi.
  • Year: 1612 - The Battle of Swally takes place, which loosens the Portuguese Empire's hold on India.
  • Year: 1549 - The papal conclave of 1549-50 begins.
  • Year: 1114 - A large earthquake damages the areas of the Crusaders in the Middle East. Antioch, Mamistra, Marash and Edessa are hit by the shocks.
  • Year: 903 - The Abbasid army under Muhammad ibn Sulayman al-Katib defeats the Qarmatians at the Battle of Hama.
  • Year: 618 - The Tang dynasty scores a decisive victory over their rival Xue Rengao at the Battle of Qianshuiyuan.
  • Year: 561 - Following the death of King Chlothar I at Compiègne, his four sons, Charibert I, Guntram, Sigebert I and Chilperic I, divide the Frankish Kingdom.
