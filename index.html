Historical Events for 01/25

  • Year: 2019 - A mining company's dam collapses in Brumadinho, Brazil, a south-eastern city, killing 270 people.
  • Year: 2015 - A clash in Mamasapano, Maguindanao in the Philippines kills 44 members of Special Action Force (SAF), at least 18 from the Moro Islamic Liberation Front and five from the Bangsamoro Islamic Freedom Fighters.
  • Year: 2013 - At least 50 people are killed and 120 people are injured in a prison riot in Barquisimeto, Venezuela.

  • Year: 1996 - Billy Bailey becomes the last person to be hanged in the United States.

  • Year: 1993 - Five people are shot outside the CIA Headquarters in Langley, Virginia. Two are killed and three wounded.
