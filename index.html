Historical Events for 03/30

  • Year: 2023 - Donald Trump becomes the first former United States president to be indicted by a grand jury.
  • Year: 2019 - Pope Francis visits Morocco.
  • Year: 2018 - The Israeli Army kills 17 Palestinians and wounds 1,400 in Gaza during Land Day protests.
  • Year: 2017 - SpaceX conducts the world's first reflight of an orbital class rocket.
  • Year: 2011 - Min Aung Hlaing is appointed as the Commander-in-Chief of Myanmar's armed forces.
  • Year: 2009 - Twelve gunmen attack the Manawan Police Academy in Lahore, Pakistan.
  • Year: 2008 - Drolma Kyi arrested by Chinese authorities.
  • Year: 2006 - Cyclone Glenda, one of the strongest tropical cyclones in the Australian region makes landfall near Onslow, Western Australia.
  • Year: 2002 - The 2002 Lyon car attack takes place.
  • Year: 1982 - Space Shuttle program: STS-3 mission is completed with the landing of Columbia at White Sands Missile Range, New Mexico.
  • Year: 1981 - U.S. President Ronald Reagan is shot in the chest outside a Washington, D.C., hotel by John Hinckley Jr.; three others are wounded in the same incident.
  • Year: 1979 - Airey Neave, a British Member of Parliament (MP), is killed by a car bomb as he exits the Palace of Westminster. The Irish National Liberation Army claims responsibility.
  • Year: 1976 - Israeli-Palestinian conflict: in the first organized response against Israeli policies by a Palestinian collective since 1948, Palestinians create the first Land Day.
  • Year: 1972 - Vietnam War: The Easter Offensive begins after North Vietnamese forces cross into the Demilitarized Zone (DMZ) of South Vietnam.
  • Year: 1967 - Delta Air Lines Flight 9877 crashes at Louis Armstrong New Orleans International Airport, killing 19.
  • Year: 1965 - Vietnam War: A car bomb explodes in front of the United States Embassy, Saigon, killing 22 and wounding 183 others.
  • Year: 1961 - The Single Convention on Narcotic Drugs is signed in New York City.
  • Year: 1959 - Tenzin Gyatso, the 14th Dalai Lama, flees Tibet for India.
  • Year: 1949 - Cold War: A riot breaks out in Austurvöllur square in Reykjavík, when Iceland joins NATO.
  • Year: 1945 - World War II: Soviet forces invade Austria and capture Vienna. Polish and Soviet forces liberate Danzig.
  • Year: 1944 - Out of 795 Lancasters, Halifaxes and Mosquitos sent to attack Nuremberg, 95 bombers do not return, making it the largest RAF Bomber Command loss of the war.
  • Year: 1944 - World War II: Allied bombers conduct their most severe bombing run on Sofia, Bulgaria.
  • Year: 1940 - Second Sino-Japanese War: Japan declares Nanking capital of a new Chinese puppet government, nominally controlled by Wang Jingwei.
  • Year: 1939 - The Heinkel He 100 fighter sets a world airspeed record of 463 mph (745 km/h).
  • Year: 1918 - Beginning of the bloody March Events in Baku and other locations of Baku Governorate.
  • Year: 1912 - Sultan Abd al-Hafid signs the Treaty of Fez, making Morocco a French protectorate.
  • Year: 1900 - Archaeologists in Knossos, Crete, discover the first clay tablet with hieroglyphic writing in a script later called Linear B.
  • Year: 1899 - German Society of Chemistry issues an invitation to other national scientific organizations to appoint delegates to the International Committee on Atomic Weights.
  • Year: 1885 - The Battle for Kushka triggers the Panjdeh Incident which nearly gives rise to war between the Russian and British Empires.
  • Year: 1870 - Texas is readmitted to the United States Congress following Reconstruction.
  • Year: 1867 - Alaska is purchased from Russia for $7.2 million, about two cents/acre ($4.19/km2), by United States Secretary of State William H. Seward.
  • Year: 1863 - Danish prince Wilhelm Georg is chosen as King George of Greece.
  • Year: 1861 - Discovery of the chemical elements: Sir William Crookes announces his discovery of thallium.
  • Year: 1856 - The Treaty of Paris is signed, ending the Crimean War.
  • Year: 1855 - Origins of the American Civil War: "Border Ruffians" from Missouri invade Kansas and force election of a pro-slavery legislature.
  • Year: 1844 - One of the most important battles of the Dominican War of Independence from Haiti takes place near the city of Santiago de los Caballeros.
  • Year: 1842 - Ether anesthesia is used for the first time, in an operation by the American surgeon Dr. Crawford Long.
  • Year: 1841 - The National Bank of Greece is founded in Athens.
  • Year: 1822 - The Florida Territory is created in the United States.
  • Year: 1818 - Physicist Augustin Fresnel reads a memoir on optical rotation to the French Academy of Sciences, reporting that when polarized light is "depolarized" by a Fresnel rhomb, its properties are preserved in any subsequent passage through an optically-rotating crystal or liquid.
  • Year: 1815 - Joachim Murat issues the Rimini Proclamation, among the earliest calls for Italian unification.
  • Year: 1699 - Guru Gobind Singh establishes the Khalsa in Anandpur Sahib, Punjab.
  • Year: 1296 - Edward I sacks Berwick-upon-Tweed, during armed conflict between Scotland and England.
  • Year: 1282 - The people of Sicily rebel against the Angevin king Charles I, in what becomes known as the Sicilian Vespers.
  • Year: 598 - Balkan Campaign: The Avars lift the siege at the Byzantine stronghold of Tomis. Their leader Bayan I retreats north of the Danube River after the Avaro-Slavic hordes are decimated by the plague.
